Privacy Policy

The Sweat Foundation Ltd is a British Virgin Islands business company (registration number 2081818) (hereafter referred to as the “Sweat Foundation”, “we” or “us” as the context may require). This Privacy Policy applies to your creation of a SWEAT Wallet via the SweatCoin mobile application (the “Sweatcoin App”), any website operated by the Sweat Foundation (the “Sweat Foundation Website(s)”) the Sweat Token mobile application (the “Sweat Token App” and together with the Sweatcoin App, the “Apps”) and the services we provide that are accessible via any of them (the “Services”). This Privacy Policy sets out the basis on which any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, will be stored and processed by us. Please note that you must be aged 18 years or older to use the Services. Please do not use the Services or provide us with any personal information if you are under 18 years of age.

INFORMATION WE MAY COLLECT FROM YOU

From time to time, we may collect or ask you to provide personal information including (without limitation) the following: your name, mobile phone number, email address, password, IP address, unique device identifiers and other identification credentials, biographical details, photographs and/or payment information.

Please note that, with your consent, we will collect information about your location and physical movements in order for the Apps to function properly and monitor and verify forms of eligible movement. You may turn location monitoring on and off from time to time using the settings of your operating system of your mobile device but, if you disable this functionality, we will not be able to collect information relating to your step-count and GPS/Cell-ID location which will prevent tracking and/or conversion of your movement into sweat tokens (“SWEAT”).

We work with third parties from time to time (including, for example, business partners, sub-contractors in technical, payment and delivery services, advertising networks, analytics providers, search information providers and credit reference agencies) who may provide to us information about you. This may include your purchase history from business partners who supply you with rewards made available via the Apps.

HOW AND WHY WE USE YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We only use your data where we have a proper reason to do so such as:

  • where you have given your consent for us to do so;
  • to rely with our legal and regulatory obligations;
  • for the performance of a contract with you or to take steps at your request before entering into a contract; or
  • or for our legitimate interests or those of a third party.

A legitimate interest is when we have a business or commercial reason to use your personal data, so long as this is not overridden by your own rights and interests. We will carry out an assessment when relying on legitimate interests, to balance our interests against your own.

Additionally, we and/or our trusted partners may contact you from time to time with offers that may interest you and/or to inform you of other products and services. All personal data we store and process is handled strictly in accordance with applicable data protection legislation.

DATA ON THE BLOCKCHAIN

In order to provide the Service, the minting of SWEAT requires certain data to be included in the data shown on the blockchain relating to the SWEAT at the time it is minted. We take steps to anonymise that data and no contact details or other personal information identifying you is included in that information. The information following is included:

  • The number of verified steps taken that caused the minting transaction to occur;
  • The amount of SWEAT received by the recipient as a result of the minting transaction caused by those verified steps;
  • The date and time on which those verified steps were converted into SWEAT by the minting transaction (but not the date and time on which those steps were actually taken);
  • The public key (unique, anonymous crypto reference) of the Wallet that received the SWEAT at the minting transaction.

Although this data is anonymous, your public key could be used to personally identify you if you provide it to a third party as any third party can read the information included in the blockchain. Neither the Sweat Foundation nor any of its affiliates control the blockchain. you should therefore assume that this data will be available publicly in perpetuity. You should therefore either avoid using the Service or avoid sharing your public key if you do not wish for people to link this information with you personally.

You may opt out of this information being included in the blockchain but in order for you to continue receiving SWEAT you must give and maintain your consent to The Sweat Foundation and its affiliates processing your data in accordance with this policy. This will not affect any historic SWEAT minting transactions. The data relating to those shall remain on the blockchain indefinitely.

For more information about blockchains and SWEAT please review our FAQs here.

COOKIES

We use cookies to distinguish you from other users of the Apps and Sweat Foundation Website(s) and obtain certain information about your usage and behaviour. A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that we put on your computer when you use the Apps or Sweat Foundation Website(s). This helps us to provide you with a better experience when you use the Apps or browse the Sweat Foundation Website(s) and also allows us to improve the Services. We will use both persistent cookies, which could remain on your device until their expiration (which, in some cases, is up to 10 years), and session cookies, which are temporary files removed from your device once your browser is closed.

The types of cookies we may use include:

  • Analytical/Tracking Cookies, which allow us to recognise and count the number of visitors and analyse use of the Services, as well as to verify transactions; and
  • Advertising and Retargeting Cookies, which allow us to generate appropriate advertising directed to you on the Sweat Foundation Website(s) as well as on the Apps and other third party websites.

Please note that third parties (including, for example, advertising networks and providers of external services like web traffic analysis services) may also use cookies, over which we have no control. These cookies are likely to be analytical/performance cookies or targeting cookies. You may block cookies by activating the appropriate setting on your browser but, if you do so, you may not be able to use all functionalities of the Services.
